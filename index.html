About me

I am a postdoc at the Departement of Computer Science, University of Bologna. I am working on the project "Complexity Analysis of Higher-Order Rewrite Systems", with kind support of the Austrian science fund (FWF) and the Focus research team.

I like to code, have a look at my software page.

I received my PhD and my MSc in Computer Science at the University of Innsbruck. For details, see my Curriculum Vitae.

Research Interests

  • First and higher-order rewrite systems
  • Implicit computational complexity
  • Automation of complexity analysis
  • Proof theory
  • Functional programming
